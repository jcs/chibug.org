CoBUG

Created in 2014, CoBUG is a completely open BSD community, established with the intention of spreading love for the BSDs.

NYC*BUG

NYC*BUG (pronounced "nice bug") was launched at a small meeting in December 2003, and officially announced at the birds-of-a-feather meeting at LinuxWorld Expo in NYC in January 2004.
