ChiBUG: Chicago Area BSD Users Group

Do you use or have any interest in FreeBSD, NetBSD, OpenBSD, or DragonflyBSD? Are you interested in meeting up with other like-minded people in the Chicago area? If so, you're in the right place!

ChiBUG meets tentatively on the second Tuesday of each month.
